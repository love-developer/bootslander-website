Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about my web development services? Here's everything you need to know before starting your project with me.

What technologies do you use for web development?

I mainly use React.js, Tailwind CSS, HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and Firebase. I also work with Shopify for ecommerce stores.

Can you convert my Figma or design into a live website?

Absolutely! Just send me your Figma or XD file and I’ll convert it into a responsive, pixel-perfect website using clean code.

Do you offer revisions or changes after delivery?

Yes, I offer free minor revisions after delivery. Major changes might cost extra depending on the scope.

Will my website be mobile responsive?

Yes! Every project I build is fully responsive — optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop screens.

Do you provide hosting or domain services?

No, I don’t directly provide hosting or domains, but I can guide you to the best platforms and help you set it up.
